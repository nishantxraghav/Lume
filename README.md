# LUME 🌙  
### A Mental Health Care App

LUME is a modern, minimal, and user-focused mental health care application designed to support emotional well-being, self-awareness, and mental clarity.  
Built with scalability and simplicity in mind, LUME aims to make mental health support accessible, private, and stigma-free.

---

## 🧠 About LUME

Mental health is just as important as physical health, yet it is often overlooked.  
LUME is created to help users track their mental state, reflect on their emotions, and build healthier habits through a calm, intuitive digital experience.

The goal is not to replace professional care, but to **assist, guide, and support** users in their mental wellness journey.

---

## ✨ Key Features (Planned)

- 📝 **Mood Tracking** – Log daily emotions and mental states  
- 📊 **Insights & Patterns** – Understand emotional trends over time  
- 🌱 **Guided Self-Care** – Simple activities for stress relief and focus  
- 🔒 **Privacy-First Design** – User data stays secure and confidential  
- 🎨 **Minimal UI/UX** – Calm, distraction-free interface  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack (Proposed)

- **Frontend:** Flutter  
- **Backend:** Python (FastAPI / Flask)  
- **Database:** Firebase / PostgreSQL  
- **ML (Future Scope):** Mood prediction & sentiment analysis
